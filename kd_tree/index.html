KD-Tree Visualizer

Enter 2D points (one per line) in the format: x y (separated by a space). For example:

13 15
6 12
9 1
2 7
10 19

After clicking "Visualize KD-Tree", use the "Next Step" button to reveal each node’s point (labeled with its name and coordinates) and then its splitting line along with its full partition region (outlined as a dashed rectangle). The KD‑tree is rendered on the right using D3. You can pan and zoom the tree visualization (scroll or drag with your mouse) to explore deeper levels.
